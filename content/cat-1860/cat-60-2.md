THERE are two divisions, in the arrangement of the Museum;-

HISTORY an NATURAl HISTORY. The specimens, illus-

trating the former, are subdivided according to the Mis-

sionary Stations from whence they have been received,

and are distinguished by the colour of the paper on

which the number is printed, as under.



GREEN.—South Sea Islands. In Cases A and C.

YELLow.—China and Ultra Ganges. Ditto DE MandI.

BLUE.—India, including the three Presidencies. Ditto F and G.

RED.—Africa and Madagascar. Ditto G H and N.

PINK.—America, North and South. Ditto O.

WHITE.—The Miscellaneous Articles, and Natural History.

Ditto B and Lobby.
