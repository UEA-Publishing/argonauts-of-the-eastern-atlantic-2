<p style="text-align:center;">CATALOGUE<br>
OF<br>
THE MISSIONARY MUSEUM,<br>
BLOMFIELD STREET, FINSBURY;<sup>1</sup></p>
<p style="text-align:center;">INCLUDING</p>>

<p style="text-align:center;">SPECIMENS IN NATURAL HISTORY,<br>
VARIOUS IDOLS OF HEATHEN NATIONS,<br>
DRESSES, MANUFACTURES, DOMESTIC UTENSILS,<br>
INSTRUMENTS OF WAR,<br>
&c. &c. &c.</p>

<p style="text-align:center;">Admission by Tickets, for a limited number of Persons, signed by a<br>
Director or Officer of the Society.</p>

---

<p style="text-align:center;">TUESDAY, THURSDAY, AND SATURDAY.</p>

---

<p style="text-align:center;">From Lady Day to Michaelmas from 10 to 4 o’clock; and from<br>
Michaelmas to Lady Day from 10 to 3 o’clock.<sup>2</sup></p>
