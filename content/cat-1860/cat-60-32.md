32
INDIAN MISSION.
[Blue.
In the Lobby.
11—17 Seven clubs used by the devil dancers in Ceylon.
18, 19 Two devil’s bows that take in parts to make them port-
able.
20—24 Two large and three small walking sticks.
25—27 Three sticks with moveable lumps.
28—31 Four jingling sticks.
32, 33 Two short sticks.
